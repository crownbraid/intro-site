Hi There,

My Name is Elynor

Introduction...

I'm a musicologist and filmmaker, with a weak spot for traditional and avant-garde creative practices. I also draw, write, curate, and teach.

Where I'm From...

I was born and raised in Hampton Roads, "America's First Region," where I also attended the oldest school in the country. This climate stimulated my interests in culture and history.

More About Me...

What I'm Working Towards...

I am trying to tend to my creative careers without capsizing the rest of my life. I hope to soon work as an artist, with enough support to budget large-scale projects.

What I Listen To...

I'm most attuned to traditional folk and contemporary art music, favoring Spectralist, xenharmonic, and electro-acoustic approaches. I like explorations of environmental sound, whether creative or explicitly documentary.

Jazz, RnB, hip-hop, and black metal are other staples of my daily listening.

Other Interests...

I'm a big fan of cartography, nature, science, and textiles.
